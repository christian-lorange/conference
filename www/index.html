FAQs

At what age does a child need to purchase a wristband?

If children are attending the festival itself and going to venues, they do need a wristband no matter how young they are, as they would still be counted towards legal occupancy at the venues. Wristbands are required for all venues, including all-ages venues, the museum, and, any non-childrens' programming. Wristbands for children are the same price as those for adults because, as part of our mission, we're trying to pay the artists fairly for playing. There is one venue for family programming that will be free during the first part of Saturday, no wristbands required, which is at Everyday Joe's. Please see the artist schedule for more information on the family programming.

Where can I buy wristbands BEFORE the festival?

Wristbands are on sale NOW through the Thursday, before the festival on the Tickets page! Or you can pay cash between April 2nd and the Thursday before the festival at Rock ‘N’ Robin’s. (Or get one for free by becoming one of our volunteers!) Wristbands purchased online will be available at the ticket sales / Will Call booth at Art Lab in Old Town at 239 Linden St. during FoCoMX.

Where can I buy wristbands the nights of the festival?

The following places will sell tickets DURING the festival: The Will Call booth in Old Town, at Art Lab at 239 LInden St, will be open Friday and Saturday from noon to 11 PM, taking cash or credit card payments. Will Call is also the only place during the festival to pick up wristbands you purchased online. A limited number of other cash physical wristband sales locations may be available, but Will Call is always your best bet with the most payment options.

Can I pay for my wristband with a credit or debit card?

Credit and debit cards will of course work online. Cash or credit/debit cards are accepted at Will Call during the festival. Only cash will be accepted for wristbands at Rock 'N' Robin's (April 1-Thursday before the festival), or at the door of the limited number of venues selling wristbands during FoCoMX.

Can I buy a wristband at the first venue I plan to attend?

Please don't count on it! We sell wristbands at only a couple of our outlying venues, but there is always a chance that venue will run out of the very short supply they are given. We recommend you buy your wristband before the festival, or during FoCoMX at Will Call (cash or credit).

If I buy my ticket online, how do I pick up my wristband?

Please note that you CANNOT pick up your Will Call wristband at the door of any venue. Will Call in Old Town is the only place during the festival to get a wristband purchased online. Open noon-11pm both Friday and Saturday.

Do I need to keep the same wristband on for both days?

YES! Your wristband is what gets you in the door at all the venues for both nights. They’re made of a durable cloth, can be worn for multiple days, and will even survive in the shower.

If I have a wristband, does it guarantee entry to any venue?

The wristband allows you entry to any venue that is participating in FoCoMX. It is not a guaranteed entry however, as maximum occupancy of venues are enforced by the city fire marshal. If there is a particular show you want to see, we encourage you to arrive at that venue early to ensure your entry.

What are the age restrictions for the festival?

See the Venues page for a listing of venues and their age restrictions. This information is also available in the Schedule for each event.

How late is the Will Call booth open?

The Will Call booth in Old Town will be open from 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM on both Friday and Saturday.

Where is the FoCoMX Will Call booth located?

Will Call will be set up at Art Lab in Old Town at 239 Linden St. in Old Town. Will Call is the only place during the festival to get a wristband purchased online.

Are there any hotel discounts this year?

Yes! While rooms last (it's a busy weekend in Fort Collins!)... The Best Western University Inn is one of our sponsors this year. We are very grateful to them for helping us keep festival costs low! Reserve your room online with our discount link, or call 970-484-1984 using the code FoCoMX. You'll get discounted $89 rooms while they last. You can also use special FoCoMX discounts at the Hilton, (Code MMX, $139), while rooms last. Or enjoy the 10% FoCoMX discount at the brand-new Elizabeth Hotel!
